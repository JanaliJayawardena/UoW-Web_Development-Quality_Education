What is Quality Education?

Quality education is a process that focuses on the whole child and prepares them for life, not just for testing. Quality education considers the social, emotional, mental, physical, and cognitive development of each student regardless of their background or location. Quality education also involves the students, teachers, environment, pedagogy, and assessment as interrelated elements that support each other. Inclusive Education Practices

Inclusive education is a practice where all students, regardless of any challenges they may have, are placed in age-appropriate general education classes that are in their own neighborhood schools to receive high-quality instruction, interventions, and supports that enable them to meet success in the core curriculum. This means the integration or inclusion of all students in the same classrooms and in the same schools, without exception, including students with special needs. A student's “particularities” should be regarded as elements of diversity instead of a problem.
